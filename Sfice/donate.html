Support Our Work

Your donations help us keep our Social Cafe running and allow us to provide essential items to those in temporary accommodation or suffering from poverty.

Physical Donations

We regularly give out food parcels and toiletries. We are currently accepting:

  • Shower gel, shampoo, and deodorant
  • Men's underwear and socks
  • Toothpaste and toothbrushes

Drop-off times are 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM, Tuesday to Thursday at SFiCE House.

Financial Donations

To make a monetary donation, please contact us or visit our main center. Every contribution goes directly towards community development.

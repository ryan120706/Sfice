Our Projects & Services

Our main mission is community development, to help reduce the effects of food poverty, isolation, and ill health within the City of Nottingham.

  • Social Cafe: We offer a free 3-course meal every Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Service users can also book showers and clothes washing.
  • Benefits Advice Cafe: Runs on Friday mornings alongside a Digital Literacy Tutor.
  • ESOL Classes: Free online and in-person English language classes.
  • Mental Health Support: Free confidential telephone listening service and support for emotional wellbeing.

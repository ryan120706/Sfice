About Us

Our History

In 2015, SFiCE opened a Social Cafe to focus on fighting food poverty in Nottingham, UK. As new issues were highlighted by Service Users, we created additional projects, including ESOL Classes, and Health & Wellbeing sessions.

Meet the Team

Our fantastic team works hard to help run our services efficiently and resourcefully. We are a dedicated group of staff and volunteers committed to community development.
